Bio

Peter McKinnon is a Canadian photographer, filmmaker, and vlogger. He is most known for his filmmaking and photography tutorials mainly using Adobe Premiere Pro or Adobe Lightroom.

He is a talented photographer who is famous for his spectacular photography. He inspires and educates his viewers about photography and cinematography on his popular channel, Peter McKinnon. He got instant limelight when he was featured in Magic Magazine and Popular Photography Magazine.
